Website 4 Headspace

Headspace is actually a website I use often because it is one of my benefits from work. This website is again very visually appealing and seems to dodge most of the common webpage mistakes in Nailsons article. But, I noticed a couple flaws in the website. First is when you enter the website you are immeditaley met with a payment option of what heapspace plan you want to buy. This isn't a common webpage mistake but I think if they changed there layout to be more information based to start so that users can understand what they are purchasing first instead of the other way around it would invoke more attention on their service they offer. The other mistake I noticed was the arrows by the payment plan option. The arrows give the illusion you can scroll through the options of headspace clicking on them but, they don't actually work. Although I do enjoy this website and think it is very well designed this would fall under on of the top ten mistakes. The mistake is "Violating User Conventions". This mistake I put under this reason because it causes usability issues by immitating arrows you can click to view your options but actually being fake buttons on the screen.
